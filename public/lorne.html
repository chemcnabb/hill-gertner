a talent-based merchant bank

Hill & Gertner Capital Corporation (Hill & Gertner) is a talent-based merchant bank headquartered in Toronto. We invest our own capital and that of third parties, and form partnerships with proven creative entrepreneurs who have extensive expertise in their respective fields. We are an agile organization driven by our belief in the entrepreneurial spirit, our ability to recognize new opportunities, and our long-standing personal relationships across all industries.

At Hill & Gertner, we embrace change and disruption, believing that it is a natural by-product of progress. When we see change, we see opportunity. More than this, we have the knowledge, know-how and financial resources to capitalize on the ever-evolving business environment. We work with retailers, manufacturers, distributors and service providers whose businesses are being affected by expansion, re-tooling, a business climate in flux, and mergers or acquisitions. Moreover, we are not intimidated by financial distress. Our strength is in our ability to act quickly and deploy our expertise and capital to maximum effect.
